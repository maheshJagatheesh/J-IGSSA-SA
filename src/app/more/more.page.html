PROVIDING SPORTING ACTIVITIES
The Hills Zone Sports Association provides a pathway to AICES, CIS and state representation for secondary students of member schools. This includes the major carnivals of swimming, cross country and athletics as well as many other representative sports. In addition, championship days and summer/winter competitions across a variety of sports provide students with the opportunity to play weekly competitive sport. HZSA also has an extensive program of primary sport including gala days and weekly competitions for Years 3-6.
